Our Facilities

As you walk around our campus, you will see a variety of academic buildings, residence halls, research centers, and recreational facilities.

World Class Library

The RV College of Engineering library is a state-of-the-art facility that provides access to a wide range of resources for the benefit of students, faculty, and researchers. The library is located on the college campus in Bangalore and has a collection of over 1,20,000 books, periodicals, and journals. The library also provides access to digital resources such as e-books, e-journals, and databases. Students and faculty members can access these resources from the library's online portal. The library also has a dedicated reading room and study area for students.

Largest Play Ground

RV College of Engineering maintained playground for students to engage in various sports and recreational activities. The college has a dedicated sports complex that includes a cricket ground, football ground, volleyball court, basketball court, and indoor sports facilities like table tennis, carrom, and chess. The playground is equipped with modern facilities and equipment, and the college encourages students to actively participate in sports and other physical activities. The college has a team of dedicated coaches who train students in various sports, and the college participates in various intercollegiate tournaments and competitions.

Tasty And Healthy

The college Food Court is designed to cater to 1000 students & staff, it is spread over 500 square meters. The college canteen is a place where students congregate to not only eat but exchange views. Our Food Court has a modern kitchen where food is steam cooked. The washed dishes are sterilized for hygienic serving. On campus we also have an extension food court counter. Apart from the Main Canteen, the Mini Canteen also caters to the needs of students and staff and is ideally located on the other side of the campus.
