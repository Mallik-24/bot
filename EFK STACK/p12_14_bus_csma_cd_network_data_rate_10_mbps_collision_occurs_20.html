      solution:

      The data rate is = 10 Mbps

      The collision occurs 20 microseconds after the first bit of the frame leaves the sending station.

      To detect the collision, the transmission delay(Tfr) must be greater than 2 x 20 = 40 microseconds.

      Hence, the length of the frame must be at least 400 bits so that the sender can detect the collision.
